Online homestay Accredition E-submission of C-Forms

Accreditation process is initiated for ensuring the quality and competence of homestays working inside the state. Now all homestays must get accreditation from the Dept of Tourism, Govt of Kerala, for its operations.

A completely online system has been instituted for accrediting Tour Operators based in Kerala. The system includes facility for online submission of applications.The accommodation providers in the state have been provided with a unique user id and password, with which they can upload details of tourist arrivals in their properties on a daily basis.
