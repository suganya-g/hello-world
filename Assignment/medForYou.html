Mission:

Med For U is committed to improving the health of the people who live in our communities by providing excellent medical care, ensuring access to that care, teaching healthy lifestyles, and working with local agencies to meet community health needs.

Vision:

We will be the regional leader in healthcare.

Community Beneit Report:

Daviess Community Hospital's mission is to improve the health of the people who live in our communities. We use a variety of methods, from screening and health education to providing charity care, to accomplish this.
